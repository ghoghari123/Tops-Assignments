My Foodie Profile

I am a food lover, and my favorite cuisine is Gujarati food. I especially enjoy a traditional Gujarati thali because it is full of delicious and comforting dishes. My favorite meal is bajrano rotlo served with ringan no olo, fresh butter, jaggery, and buttermilk. One of my best food memories was enjoying this meal with my family during the winter season. The smoky flavor of the olo and the warm, soft rotlo made the meal unforgettable. Sharing it with my family made it even more special, and it remains my favorite meal to this day
